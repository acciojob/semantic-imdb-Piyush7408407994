Plot Summary

In a dystopian future, Earth is slowly becoming uninhabitable due to a global blight. Humanity's last hope rests on a team of astronauts who travel through a wormhole near Saturn in search of a new habitable planet. Led by former NASA pilot Joseph Cooper, they embark on an extraordinary interstellar voyage, grappling with the vastness of space, the complexities of time dilation, and the profound sacrifices required to save humankind. The film explores themes of love, time, survival, and the human spirit's relentless drive to explore and endure.
