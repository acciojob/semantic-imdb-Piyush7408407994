Summary

Interstellar is a science fiction epic that follows a group of astronauts who travel through a wormhole near Saturn in search of a new home for humanity as Earth faces ecological collapse. The story blends emotional human drama with mind-bending scientific concepts, exploring themes of love, time, and survival.
